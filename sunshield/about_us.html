Who We Are

Our website is dedicated to helping migrant families protect their skin health in the Australian sun. We offer real-time UVindex, personalized sun protection recommendations, skincancer detection tools, and interactive skin health education games. With these features, we help you and your family understand and take effective protective measures to ensure that you keep your skin safe and healthy while enjoying the sun.
