Sunscreen

Choose a broad-spectrum sunscreen with SPF 30+, apply evenly 15-30 minutes before going out, covering all exposed skin, and reapply every two hours, especially after swimming or sweating

Sun Protection Clothes

Sun protective clothing with UPF 50+ effectively blocks most UV rays. Opt for long sleeves and lightweight, breathable fabric for full-body protection in hot weather

Sun hat

A sun hat is an effective UV protection. Choose a wide-brimmed hat (at least 7.5 cm) to cover your face, ears, and neck. Ensure it's made from densely woven material to block UV rays

Find The Shade

Finding shade is a simple, effective way to avoid direct sun exposure. Prioritize shaded areas, especially during peak UV times, to significantly reduce the risk of sunburn

Sunglasses

Sunglasses with UV400 or 100% UV protection effectively block UVA and UVB rays. Wrap-around frames offer better protection by blocking UV rays from the sides
